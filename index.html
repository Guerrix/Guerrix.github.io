About Me

I have over 9 years of experience doing iOS Apps for the entertainment, sports and social media industries, 5 of which working with in Objective-C and 4+ working with Swift.

I consider myself a passionate, self-taught and resourceful iOS Engineer, I truly love what I do and enjoy doing it. I like to “Get Things Done”. Always trying to deliver on time, following the best practices in order to have the best code quality possible.

I have been lucky enough to collaborate and participate in a wide variety of iOS projects and meet great and talented iOS Engineers from which I have absorbed as much knowledge as possible to improve my skills and improve my ways to approach daily work challenges. I love to learn and contribute in any and every possible way.

I work closely with clients to define requirements, write design documents and technical specifications, sharing also my expertise in the Apple approval & distribution Process.

I live in Hermosillo, Sonora, Mexico.
